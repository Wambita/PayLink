PayLink

Borderless payments for everyone, powered by Stellar

What is PayLink?

PayLink is a lightweight tool that helps freelancers, creators, and small businesses receive payments globally using Stellar. No banks, no borders — just fast, affordable transactions in seconds.

How it Works

  • Enter your Stellar public wallet address
  • Specify amount to receive (XLM)
  • Generate and share a payment link
  • Get notified instantly when you're paid

Why Stellar?

  • Near-zero transaction fees
  • Fast settlement in 2–5 seconds
  • Support for XLM and stablecoins like USDC
  • Open, transparent, and scalable
